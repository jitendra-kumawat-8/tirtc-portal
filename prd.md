1. HERO SECTION

Title
Telecom Innovation, Research & Training Centre (TIRTC)

Subtitle
BRBRAITT Campus, Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh

Description
An industry-led initiative anchored by the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII), in collaboration with the Department of Telecommunications (DoT).

Creating a dedicated institutional platform for telecom skilling, innovation, applied research and workforce development.

Strong placement and hiring integration 3. BACKGROUND (PROBLEM)

India’s telecom ecosystem is rapidly evolving towards AI-driven, cloud-based, software-defined and secure 5G/6G infrastructure.

This shift is creating demand for:

Network and field engineers
Cybersecurity specialists
Cloud and data centre professionals
Automation and optimisation experts

However, industry-ready talent remains a major gap.

4. OBJECTIVE (SOLUTION)
   Build industry-aligned training infrastructure
   Equip youth with job-ready telecom skills
   Enable employment and entrepreneurship
   Strengthen telecom innovation ecosystem
   Create a sustainable talent pipeline
